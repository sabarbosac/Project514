    WINTER ROUTE FINDER

    Background

    The purpose of this app is to compute the best route to take on snowy winter days.Â  The route needs to combine efficiency (quickest path) and safety (clear roads). The reason to choose this project is because in winter is really dangerous to drive and this app would help people to move from one place to another by the safest route decreasing the car accidents.



    Instructions

    1. A first Click in the map will be the initial point (The coordinate will be show in the initial coordinates box)
    2. A second Click in the map will be the final point (The coordinate will be show in the final coordinates box)
    3. A third Click in the red points (deployment stations) will show the attributes (The suggestion is to select the closest station in the route)
    4. Once you know the station, select it.
    5. Click in the submit button and the route will appear
    6. Finally, Use the Reset button to clean the map.
